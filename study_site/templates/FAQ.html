Frequently Asked Questions
How do I make a study group?

UniTea makes creating a study group a very simple and streamlined experience.

1. First, register for UniTea and then log in. Once logged in, you will be directed to the UniTea home page.

2. From there, click the create group button near the bottom of the UniTea homepage.

3. When creating a study group, input a group name, description, and a subject that you desire.

4. Once you are satisfied with your group's details, click the submit button and your study group will now be created!

How do I delete a study group?

Here at UniTea, we understand that nothing lasts forever. This is why we make deleting your study group quick and easy. If you are the creator of a study group and you wish to remove your group from our site, simply follow these steps:

1. Navigate to your study group that you wish to delete.

2. In your study group page you will a edit group option next to your username, click it.

3. Below all of the edit options for your study group you will see a delete button, click this button and your study group will then be deleted.

Keep in mind that all deletions from UniTea are final and can not be reversed.

How many members can be in a study group?

UniTea currently supports 20 members per study group. Team work does make the dream work, that is why we want groups to be large enough to support teams but also condensed enough to help facilitate their success.

How do I view and manage my study groups?

To view all your study groups that you joined and host you can follow these steps:

1. Click the home button in the navigation bar

2. You should see a dashboard on the top of the page. Click on the "See All Groups button" below the dashboard.

3. Now you should be able to view groups that you joined and hosted separately. Or even leave a joined group or delete a group you created.

How do I make a post?

To make a post for the Main Forum or a study group forum:
Main Forum

1. Click on the Forumn tab in the navigation bar.

2. Then click on the "+ Create a post" button.

3. Enter the required fields and click the submit button.

Study group forum
1. Navigate to the Home page.

2. Find the desired study group in your dashboard, either one of the first few displayed on the page or by clicking the "See All Groups"

3. Under the forum section of the study group, click on the "+ Create a post" button.

4. Enter the required fields for the post and then click the submit button.

How do I reply to a post?

To reply to a post in the Main Forum or a study group forum:
Main Forum

1. Click on the Forumn tab in the navigation bar.

2. Click on the post you wish to reply to

3. Navigate to the input field, type your message, then click "Send"

Study group forum
1. Navigate to the Home page.

2. Find the desired study group in your dashboard, either one of the first few displayed on the page or by clicking the "See All Groups"

3. Under the forum section of the study group, click on the post you want to reply to.

4. Enter a message in the input field in the comment section and click "Send"

How do I view my profile?

To view your profile, hover over your name in the navigation bar and click on it.

Can I delete my account?

Yes you are able to delete your account. The steps to delete your account are:

1. Navigate to your profile, by clicking your name on the navigation bar.

2. Select "edit user profile" under your profile picture.

3. Now click the delete button to delete your account.

We are deeply sadden to see you go if you decide to delete your account.
