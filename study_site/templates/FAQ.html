Frequently Asked Questions
How do I make a study group?

UniTea makes creating a study group a very simple and streamlined experience. First, register for UniTea and then log in. Once logged in, you will be directed to the UniTea home page. From there, click the create group button near the bottom of the UniTea homepage. When creating your study group, you will have the option to customize the group name, description, and subject. Once you are satisfied with your group's details, click the submit button and your study group will now be created!

How do I delete a study group?

Here at UniTea, we understand that nothing can last forever. This is why we make deleting a study group quick and easy. If you are the creator of a study group and you wish to remove it from our site, simply follow these steps. First, navigate to your study group that you wish to delete. You will see an edit group option next to your username in the study group, click it. Below all the edit options for the study group you will see a delete button, click this button and your study group will be deleted. Please think this through carefully, as all deletions from UniTea are final and can not be reversed.
